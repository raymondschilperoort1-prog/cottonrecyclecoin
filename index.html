Why CottonRecycleCoin?

The textile industry generates massive waste and lacks transparent incentives for recycling. CRC tokenizes recycled cotton and rewards real‑world circularity using blockchain proofs integrated with a next‑gen mechanical recycling facility in the Netherlands.

Burn Mechanism & Value Growth

  • Transactional burn: 0.5% of every CRC transaction is sent to 0x000...dead.
  • Factory‑linked burn: per 1,000 kg recycled cotton, 1% of the CRC used is burned.
  • Impact: decreasing supply + growing demand creates structural scarcity.

Result: a self‑reinforcing, deflationary economy that protects long‑term holder value while tying token scarcity to real‑world recycling throughput.
